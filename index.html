What We Do:
WildTide provides the opportunity for girls and boys to play lacrosse - ranging from the first time you hold a stick to support for college recruitment.

Mission:
To support youth lacrosse players, empowering well-rounded individuals who strengthen our community with the skills and lessons for success in life.

Who We Are:
WildTide is the premiere youth travel lacrosse organization for Brunswick County, North Carolina serving as a part of the Brunswick County Youth Council, a 501(3)c nonprofit organization.

Age Groups We Support:
Currently we have opportunities for High School Girls and Boys, Middle School Girls and Boys, and Girls and Boys Developmental K-4.
